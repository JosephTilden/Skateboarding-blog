Famous skaters

Skateboarding can be a very competitive platform. Many people try to make a living through it but few find much success. Those who manage to support themselves through it normally get a lot of media exposure. This article will go through some of the most popular skateboarders of today.

Tony Hawk

Tony Hawk has been a household name for many years now and for good reason. You may know him from tv, from his video games, or just in conversation, but you'd have a hard time finding someone who hasn't heard of him. He's most famous for performing the first ever 900, where he makes two and half rotations in the air on a skateboard. This shot him into popularity right as the game Tony Hawk Pro Skater was released, so he became even better known. I'm actually in the middle of playing the second game in the installment.

Bam Margera

Bam Margera, well known as a member of the Jackass movies, originally made a name for himself through skateboarding. He made skateboarding films with his friends but included a bunch of non-skateboarding tricks and stunts. The group got realized by MTV, which is how Jackass was born.

Leticia Bufoni

Leticia Bufoni is a super successful Brazilian skateboarder recognized for her performance in competitions. She has six X Games medals and numerous Dew Tour results under her belt. She even finished 1st in the 2015 Street League. She's well known for trying some pretty dangerous tricks that her competitors wouldn't even think of pulling.
